The Magic of Thanks Giving!

By Cibele Baer

In United States, close to the end of the year, there is a celebration called "ThanksGiving". In ThanksGiving as the name suggest, we celebrate and "say thank you" for something that we have, something that we achived or, something that we felt during the year, or our whole lives.

Thanks giving is something very special for me and my family, because we can sit together and talk, laugh, or cry with our emotions. We all had a very difficult year, because of the COVID-19, but that does not mean that everything was bad, and we should be thankful for being able to support all of the events that happended.

So, I want to invite everybody from the weather moment, to remember and share, what were you grateful for this year, it doesn't matter if you're from a different place with a different culture, we all should remember to be grateful!.
