Just Take a Picture!

By Larissa

There's so many beautiful things in this world, and a lot of times we just ignore it, beauty can be seen in almost everything in the world, we just need to pay more attention to our surroundings.

Not too long ago, I started to look at the pictures of the gallary of the Weather Moment page, and after that, I felt inspired to also start to take pictures of things, places, or people. Because of this action, I started to look to the world with different eyes! Taking pictures changed my perspective of the world!

The happiness that I feel every time I took a picture, is really vicious, so be careful to not get addicted as well :D
