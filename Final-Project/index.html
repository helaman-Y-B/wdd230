ADAMA

Adama is a company in Londrina, that sells and assist cultivators in protecting their products from pests, temporals and air aplications.

Adama is one of the mundial liders in protection of cultivation, and started your busisness in 1945 in Israel.
But in Brazil the history started in 1970, when an Herbitechnic was opened in Londrina, and after a few years, the Herbitechnic got sold to the israel company, and became what we see today, ADAMA Company.

Cacique

Cacique was founded in 1959 in Londrina by Horácio Sabino Coimbra, and sice that year, the company has been growing continuously, and today it's one of the biggest industries of instant coffe production in the world!

Today they produce everyday, 90 tons of instant coffee, and it's present in more then 70 different countries.

A.Yoshii

The company was founded in 1965 in a city called Apucarana, but ten years after being in the market, it decided to transfer it's company to Londrina.

Today A.Yoshii is present in almost every corner of the country, with the construction of buildings, schools, universities, teathers, and many other things. In total they constructed more than two million cubic meters in all the country!
