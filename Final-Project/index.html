The Purpose of Little London Commerce

The purpose of the “Little London Commerce” is to increase the awareness of the people from all around the world, about the commerce of the city.

We also want to show that Londrina is not just for commerce, but also for tourists, with a lot of different events and fun things to do during the year.

Londrina have a big potential in a lot of different market fields of our world, and and because of that potential, we created Little London Commerce, so that we can raise the curiosity and raise the interest of other companies, to work within the city.

We assure you, that investing in Londrina will only bring profit for your company!

City information

Londrina was originally explored by British settlers, and then officially established in 1930 by a small group of Italian, Japanese and German settlers. It rapidly became the commercial, political, and cultural center of the state's northern pioneer zone.

Today Londrina has achieved the incredible mark of 575.377 habitants in 2020, it's a growth of 0,99% in relation to the 2019 growth. There are almost 70 thousand more people in ten years!

Local Companies

Cacique

Cacique was founded in 1959 in Londrina by Horácio Sabino Coimbra, and since that year, the company has been growing continuously, and today it's one of the biggest industries of instant coffee production in the world!

Today they produce every day, 90 tons of instant coffee, and it's present in more then 70 different countries.

ADAMA

Adama is a company in Londrina, that sells and assist cultivators in protecting their products from pests, different types of weather and air applications.

Adama is one of the world leaders in protection of cultivation, and started your business in 1945 in Israel.
But in Brazil the history started in 1970, when an Herbitechnic was opened in Londrina, and after a few years, the Herbitechnic got sold to the Israel company, and became what we see today, ADAMA Company.

A.Yoshii

The company was founded in 1965 in a city called Apucarana, but ten years after being in the market, it decided to transfer it's company to Londrina.

Today A.Yoshii is present in almost every corner of the country, with the construction of buildings, schools, universities, theaters, and many other things. In total they constructed more than two million cubic meters in all the country!

Subscriptions

We from the Little London Commerce have memberships subscriptions, which brings a lot of different benefits and perks for your company.

We currently have 4 different types of annual subscriptions Non-profit, Bronze, Silver and Gold.

Non-profit subscriptions are perfect for business that had just entered the market, the subscription gives the company Monthly Newsletter about news of Londrina, Access to Commerce Events, Educational Opportunities and Concierge Service at City Hall.

Bronze are best for companies that just moved to Londrina, it gives all the features from Non-profit subscription plus a chance to be featured in the Newsletter every year.

Silver gives all the benefits from Bronze plus a chance to promote your business in our online directory 4 times per year. It is recommended for business that are looking for investors or partners to work with them.

Gold gives all the benefits from silver but instead of an 4 time chance to promote your business in our online directory, it becomes a monthly feature, gold also gives you an opportunity to get connected with a seasoned business owner in your industry to get real-life advice from someone who's been there before.
