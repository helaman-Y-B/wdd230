The History

The history of Londrina started in 1929 as the first advanced post of an english project. In 29 of august, the first expediction from the Company called, "Companhia de Terras Norte do Paraná", in an denominated area called "Patrimônio Três Bocas", was where the enginer Alexandre Razgulaeff established the first landmark in the lands where Londrina would appear.
